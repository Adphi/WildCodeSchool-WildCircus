About Time

A poetic time travel through the re-creation of seasons

Once upon a time, time disappeared.
Two worlds were created.
One in the light, one in the dark.

Between them, there was a tiny little space, preserved from the ancient world full of ancient stuffs, where two tiny little guys were trapped with a key.
One day, a book fell.

Follow the clowns in the book of Times

Pitzy and Gidzy will take an exentric and touching path through many adventures to fixe the time.

ABOUT

Since 1978 the Wild Circus Company never stopped renewing the circus arts through multidisciplinarity, poetic and moving performances.

Always striving for artistic perfection, they rapidly became a leading company in contemporary circus arts.

To date, more than 5,000 events have taken place.
